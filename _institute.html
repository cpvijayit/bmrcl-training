BMRC Training Institude

The Training Institute was established on 8th September, 2011.

The Training Institute is a multi-disciplinary organization specializing in capacity building, consultancy and research support, particularly for the Metro Technology Instituted at a cost of 22 corers with a vision Bangalore centre as the International Training hub in Metro Technologies.
